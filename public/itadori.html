Yuji Itadori

“I want to save people so they can have a proper death.”

Yuji Itadori is a brave and compassionate sorcerer whose journey begins after becoming the vessel of Sukuna. Even under impossible pressure, he keeps moving forward.

  • Role: Jujutsu Sorcerer
  • Strength: Superhuman physical ability
  • Nature: Kind, fearless, determined
  • Conflict: Vessel of Sukuna

Yuji’s greatest strength is not only power, but his heart. He fights for others, even when the pain becomes unbearable.
