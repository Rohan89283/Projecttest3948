Jujutsu Sorcerer

Yuji Itadori

“I want to save people so they can have a proper death.”

Yuji Itadori is brave, compassionate, and physically gifted beyond normal human limits. After becoming the vessel of Sukuna, his journey becomes one of pain, duty, and courage.

Strength Superhuman body
Nature Kind-hearted
Conflict Sukuna’s vessel
Presence Fearless

Yuji’s true power is more than physical force. It comes from his heart, his loyalty, and his refusal to look away when people need saving.

The heart of the story

Itadori represents humanity inside chaos. His orange-red theme reflects heat, motion, emotion, and inner strength — the energy of someone who keeps moving forward even when the burden is unbearable.

Theme feeling

This page is designed to feel energetic, fiery, and human. The warm glow reflects Itadori’s courage, pain, and determination to do what is right.
