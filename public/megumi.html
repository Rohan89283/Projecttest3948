Megumi Fushiguro

“I choose who I save.”

Megumi Fushiguro is a calm, intelligent sorcerer with a quiet but powerful presence. He fights with strategy, discipline, and deep inner conviction.

  • Role: Jujutsu Sorcerer
  • Technique: Ten Shadows Technique
  • Style: Tactical and composed
  • Nature: Serious, loyal, reserved

Megumi’s strength lies in balance — control, intelligence, and hidden intensity. He is one of the most compelling sorcerers in the story.
