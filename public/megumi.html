Megumi Fushiguro

“I choose who I save.”

Megumi Fushiguro is calm, intelligent, and highly tactical. His Ten Shadows Technique makes him one of the most dangerous and mysterious sorcerers of his generation.

Technique Ten Shadows
Nature Reserved
Style Tactical combat
Presence Quiet intensity

Megumi’s strength comes from judgment, discipline, and control. He does not waste motion or emotion, making him feel sharp, dangerous, and deeply focused.
