Satoru Gojo

“Throughout heaven and earth, I alone am the honored one.”

Satoru Gojo is the strongest jujutsu sorcerer of the modern era. Gifted with the Six Eyes and the Limitless technique, he possesses unmatched control, perception, speed, and cursed energy mastery.

Technique Limitless
Ability Six Eyes
Role Teacher & Sorcerer
Vibe Calm, untouchable, elite

Beneath his playful confidence lies a man determined to change the jujutsu world and protect the next generation from the failures of the old system.

Why Gojo stands above all

Gojo’s presence is overwhelming because he combines genius-level battle sense with absurd cursed technique control.
