Gojo Satoru

“Throughout heaven and earth, I alone am the honored one.”

Gojo Satoru is known as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer. With immense cursed energy, the Limitless technique, and the Six Eyes, he stands in a league of his own.

  • Role: Special Grade Sorcerer
  • Technique: Limitless
  • Ability: Six Eyes
  • Style: Confident, playful, unstoppable

Gojo represents absolute power, intelligence, and charisma. He fights to reshape the jujutsu world and protect the next generation.
